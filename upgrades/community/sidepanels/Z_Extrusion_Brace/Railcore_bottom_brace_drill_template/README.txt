# Summary

This template is used to drill the holes in the railcore bottom panel to space the extra base extrusions in from the sides.

It will yield a 40mm spacing between the outside extrusion and the added extrusions.  Place it all the way into the corner for the two outside end holes, and then measure the other 3 holes at approximately 90mm intervals.
