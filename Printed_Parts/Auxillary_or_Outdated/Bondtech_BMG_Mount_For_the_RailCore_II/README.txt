# Summary

This mount will let you use a Bondtech BMG on your Railcore II instead of the E3D Titan.  Bolts on the same (has a spot for an optional brass insert to be added in the Y carriage for extra stability - to do so, drill a hole in your Y carriage and add a brass insert)

Use with the "standard" version of the Bondtech BMG.

(Originally designed by Roy Sun)

The Fusion 360 design is available here: https://a360.co/2qeLw2S
